The Kenyan History Channel

Kenya has ahad a history but not most know about it. The challenge is that with technology moving so fast it has become a great task foe for millenials to go back to where we all started. This is a blog to immortalize history and promote awareness on it. We will be taking you on a journey to the past through pictures and videos. Some videos will not have sound for technological reasons of the time.Let's take a journey in time.
